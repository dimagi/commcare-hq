You’ve recently decided to try your hand at growing pumpkins – right in time for the fall harvest!

You want to keep track of how well your pumpkins grow this summer. You’ve decided to keep detailed paper logs of the pumpkins’ growth.

  • Your pumpkin’s ID number (you love your pumpkins, so you tagged them all!)

  • The amount of water you gave it that day (measured in liters)
