Tools for a New Community Health Workforce

90,000+ patient visits - 20 unique organizations - over 10 countries worldwide

What is CommCare?

  • A free and open-source software application with mobile and server-side components
  • A job aid for community health workers (CHWs)
  • A tool for supervision and evidence-based change
  • A way to capture data in an electronic repository that otherwise sits in thousands of paper notebooks

Why CommCare?

  • CHWs have demonstrated a potential to substantially improve health outcomes in areas of extreme poverty
  • CHWs tend to have little training and operate independently
  • CommCare provides phone-based tools that help support and manage large numbers of CHWs
  • CommCare can be an educational tool and portable reference manual allowing CHWs to deliver better care

Highlights

  • Runs on a variety of cell phones and devices, from Nokia to Android
  • Provides portable case management, tracking patients and outcomes over time
  • Supports image and audio prompts
  • Handles multiple languages and scripts including English, Spanish, Swahili, Hindi, and Dari
  • Is fully customizable, without paying a technical team
